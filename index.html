How to Use

  1. Camera Scan: Click "Start Camera", allow camera access, and point at a QR code.
  2. Image Upload: Click the upload area or drag & drop an image containing a QR code.
  3. Click "Scan QR Code" to process the uploaded image.
  4. Use the "Copy Text" button to copy the result to your clipboard.
  5. Switch between camera and upload modes using the tabs.
